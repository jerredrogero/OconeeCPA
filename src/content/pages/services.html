Our Services

Oconee CPA provides a variety of services for small businesses and individuals, customized for each client's needs. Our expertise spans across tax compliance, financial accounting, payroll services, and business consulting, with specialized experience in real estate.

  • Income Tax Compliance & Planning

    • Preparation of Federal and State Business, Fiduciary and Individual Tax Returns • Income Tax Planning and Projections • Extension and Estimated Payment Calculations • Federal and/or State Tax Resolution

  • Client Accounting Services

    • General Bookkeeping Support (Monthly or Quarterly) • Financial Statement Compilations prepared in accordance with the Statements on Standards for Accounting and Review Services (SSARS)

  • Payroll & Payroll Tax Compliance

    • Payroll Setup and Processing • Preparation of Federal and State Payroll Tax Returns (Quarterly and Annual)

  • Business Advisory Services

    • New Business Formation and Entity Selection • Business Planning - Budgeting and Cash Flow Management • QuickBooks Setup and Training • Real Estate Financial Modeling and Forecasting • Real Estate Transaction Assistance (i.e., Due Diligence and Underwriting) • Real Property Tax Consulting & Appeal Advisory
